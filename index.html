Operational & Performance Challenges

Based on the performance data and campaign execution experience, the following issues are limiting growth efficiency and engagement quality.

Engagement & Responsiveness

  • Ads sometimes continue into weekends, leaving inquiries unreplied and reducing daily engagement.
  • During ad runs, inbound volume can overwhelm a single responder; customer responses need delegation to a secondary party.
  • No consistent internet throughout the week, causing delayed responses and monitoring gaps.

Tracking & Infrastructure

  • Poor return-on-investment tracking; recommend implementing POS or digital invoicing for clear attribution.
  • Creative tooling is personal (Canva personal account); need a designated Theotoka Canva account for ownership, governance, and cost tracking.
  • Analytics constraints on Twitter limit insight; platform remains monitored but under-instrumented.

Data-Observed Risks

  • Weekend response gaps during paid campaigns risk lower conversion despite strong weekday interactions.
  • Lead and messaging spikes during ads stress single-thread handling; delayed replies reduce close rates.
  • ROI visibility is limited without POS/digital invoicing integration, making optimization and budget defense harder.
  • Creative asset ownership and consistency risk persists while relying on a personal Canva account.
